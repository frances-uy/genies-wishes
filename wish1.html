You Chose Wealth!

The genie grants you endless wealth. You become the richest person in the world overnight.

In an instant, you find yourself in a lavish mansion, its halls lined with gold and precious gems. Servants attend to your every whim, and outside, a fleet of luxury cars awaits. You have become the richest person in the world, literally overnight.

Your days are now filled with opulence and extravagance. Every imaginable luxury is at your fingertips, and your newfound wealth opens doors that were previously unfathomable. People from all walks of life seek your company, and your influence stretches across nations. You can buy anything, travel anywhere, and indulge in every pleasure.

However, as the days pass, you realize that wealth isn't just luxury and ease. It comes with its own set of challenges and decisions for a meaningful life.

What do you choose to do with all your money?
